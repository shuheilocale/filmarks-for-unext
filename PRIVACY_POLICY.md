# Privacy Policy — U-NEXT Ratings

Last updated: 2026-03-01

## Overview

U-NEXT Ratings is a Chrome extension that displays movie review scores from Filmarks and Rotten Tomatoes on U-NEXT movie detail pages.

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## How It Works

1. The extension reads the movie title from the U-NEXT page you are viewing.
2. It sends the movie title to Filmarks (filmarks.com) and Rotten Tomatoes (rottentomatoes.com) to retrieve publicly available review scores.
3. The scores are displayed directly on the page.

All network requests are made solely to retrieve movie scores. No data is sent to any other server or third party.

## Data Storage

This extension does not use cookies, local storage, or any other storage mechanism. No data is persisted.

## Third-Party Services

- **Filmarks** (filmarks.com) — used to search for movie scores and English titles.
- **Rotten Tomatoes** (rottentomatoes.com) — used to retrieve critic and audience scores.

These services are accessed only to fetch publicly available movie information. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle requests.

## Analytics and Tracking

This extension does not use any analytics, tracking, or telemetry.
